What do we mean by Archiving?: What Islam Hub (we) mean by Archiving is the reproduction of the basic level (stripped down) or whole level (not stripped, may include CSS or JS) pages, PDFs, and other files or media of an Islamic Website. We will then host it on our own website using a SSG (static site generator) such as github.io or directly on our website. But it doesn't just have to be a website, it could be an application, or another type of resource.

Who do we archive?: We will archive any resource if they meet the following requirements: a) they have not already been archived | b) they give us permission to archive there resource(s) | c) we are able to archive them (we will try multiple times to do so) | d) someone or a group of people, or the resource themselves has given us a request to archive them

When do we archive?: Usually an archive will take a few days to process / begin, or depending on the size and other factors, it may take a 2-3 weeks to begin. Once it does begin however, we will try to archive as much as we can. If you have a latest backup (snapshot or any other form) of your resource(s) which you are comfortable sharing and that is compatible for archiving, then please provide us access with it so that we can use that instead.

How do we archive?: We download the pages, edit them, make sure they are functional (if they have CSS or Java Script we make sure those work as well / they are backed up on our side) and then we check through them for the final time, and then we publish them on there archive.

Where do we archive?: For websites, we use an SSG (Static Site Generator) such as github.io or gitlab.io and host them under a subdomain. For other resources they end up in: library or archives hub.
